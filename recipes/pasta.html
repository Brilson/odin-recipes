Description

This is a simple Pasta Recipe that works for almost any type of pasta!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Salt the water slightly
  3. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook for 10 minutes
  4. Drain most of the water
  5. Add the sauce to the pasta and stir on low heat for a couple of minutes
  6. Add cheese and serve
